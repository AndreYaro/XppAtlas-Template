# Cleanup Candidates

Non-destructive audit list. Nothing here is auto-deleted. Each entry is something a human should confirm and remove manually.

Format per entry:

```
### <short title>
- Path: <absolute or repo-relative path>
- Why it looks stale: <one or two sentences>
- Suggested action: <delete / archive / merge-into-X / ask-owner>
- Flagged on: <YYYY-MM-DD> by <session>
```

Entries are appended, not rewritten. Once the user confirms an item is handled, move it under `## Resolved` with the resolution date.
